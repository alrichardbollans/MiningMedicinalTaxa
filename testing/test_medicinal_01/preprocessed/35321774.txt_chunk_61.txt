Flagellumshelp the bacteria to find the target cell efficiently in the stomach. Thebacteria adheres to the surface of epithelial cells. With the help ofurease which is produced by the bacteria, it breaks down the urea,which is normally produced in the stomach. This process releasescarbon dioxide and ammonia.
